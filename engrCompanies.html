What types of companies hire engineers?

  1. BMW Group
         BMW Group is a German company, and is one of the worlds leading automotive manufacturers. With more than 100,000 employees in over 100 counties BMW Group is also a very diverse company. BMW Group set really high standards in terms of individual mobility and in order to continue to meet these standards now and in the future, BMW Group wants to have the best employees possible. For BMW Group this means that more than just good grades are needed for employment. BMW Group wants employees that carry out their work with a great deal of enthusiasm and team spirit, because job satisfaction is the foundation for personal and business success.
         As an engineer working for BMW Group the average salary is $89,295. BMW Group engineers work mainly in Germany and the USA at manufacturing and research locations. As an engineer for BMW Group one does a lot of group work and are employed full time. BMW Group encourages engineers to balance work and their life productively. They also encourage engineers to exercise and to do sports. BMW Group also offers support with child care for employees.

  2. Subaru
         Subaru is the automobile manufacturing division of the Japanese company Fuji Heavy Industries (FHI). With more than 100,000 employees Subaru is an international and diverse company. Subaru prides its self in giving back to the community which they do through many different projects that support helping out the environment, and local area. Subaru believes in protecting the environment and has enacted policies including recycling, reducing harmful emissions, and educating their employees on the environment. Subaru has also created an assembly plant which has a zero landfill status. This means that nothing from the manufacturing process goes into a landfill.
         As an engineer working for Subaru the average salary is $83,100. Subaru engineers work mainly in the USA and Japan at research and manufacturing plants. Subaru encourages their engineers to stay creative in their work place. They offer flexible hours and a casual dress code to make work feel more like home. They offer two weeks of vacation annually and engineers get paid time off when they volunteer. Engineers are encouraged to perform community service and to give back to their local community.
