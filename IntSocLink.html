Interesting Professional Society

The American Society of Mechanical Engineers is a Professional Society that interests me as a future engineer. I want to become a mechanical engineer in the future and being part of this society would provide me with a large number of benefits. Some benefits include networking, and conferences. I want to be successful with whatever I do and I truly believe that by joining a society such as this one I can get closer to my goal.
